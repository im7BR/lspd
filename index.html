Towing a Vehicle: Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to correctly tow and impound a vehicle.

Step 1: Find Vehicle

Locate the vehicle that needs to be towed.

Step 2: Check PDA for Fines

Check the vehicle through the PDA and make sure the owner didn't get a fine for the same violation earlier today.

Step 3: Log Plates/VIN (Screenshot)

Type one of the following commands in chat and take a screenshot.

If plates are visible:

/me Searches the plates through the LSPD database and writes them down.

If no plates are visible:

/me Kneels down, feels the VIN number under the car and run it through LSPD.

Step 4: Check Plate in PDA (Screenshot)

Check the license plate in the PDA and take a screenshot.

If there is no license plate, check the owner's name and take a screenshot of that.

Step 5: Check Fines (Screenshot)

Check the owner's fine history in the PDA and take a screenshot.

Step 6: Request Tow

Press G on the vehicle and select "Towing vehicle".

Step 7: Photograph Vehicle (Screenshot)

Take a picture of the vehicle with your phone.

Before saving, take a screenshot of the photo preview. Then, save the photo to the PDA.

Step 8: Hook Vehicle

Get in your tow truck.

While holding CTRL, back up to the vehicle to hook it.

Once attached, hold SHIFT to lift it.

Step 9: Transport to Impound

Start driving to the penal parking area next to LSPD HQ.

Step 10: Issue Fine & Impound

Before going into the yellow impound zone, get out and check the vehicle's state value (damage).

You are only allowed to give 10% of the vehicle's state value as a fine.

Max Fine: If 10% is more than $20,000, you must lower the fine to $20,000.

Type the reason for the tow/fine and complete the impound.

Procedure Complete

You have successfully completed the towing procedure. You can now return to the home screen.

Fine Vehicle: Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to correctly issue a vehicle fine.

Step 1: Find Vehicle

Locate the vehicle associated with the violation.

Step 2: Check Owner's Daily Fines

Check the owner of the vehicle in the PDA. Make sure they haven't already received a fine for the same violation today. If they have, you cannot issue another fine for it.

Step 3: Log Plates/VIN (Screenshot)

Type one of the following commands in chat and take a screenshot.

If plates are visible:

/me Searches the plates through the LSPD database and writes them down.

If no plates are visible:

/me Kneels down, feels the VIN number under the car and run it through LSPD.

Step 4: Check Plate/Owner in PDA (Screenshot)

Check the license plate in the PDA and take a screenshot.

If there is no license plate, check the owner's name and take a screenshot of that.

Step 5: Check Owner's Fines (Screenshot)

Check the owner's fine history in the PDA and take a screenshot.

Step 6: Issue Fine

Click the button "Issue a Fine" in the PDA and fill in the details for the violation.

Step 7: Screenshot Confirmation

After issuing the fine, take a screenshot of the confirmation screen or the updated fine history showing the new fine.

Procedure Complete

The fine has been issued and documented.

About This Tool

This tool was developed by a fellow officer to streamline common LSPD procedures and provide quick access to the Penal Code guide.

The goal is to help new and experienced officers alike perform their duties efficiently and accurately, reducing confusion and saving time during critical moments. Whether you need to look up a charge, follow towing procedures, or manage your duty status, this aims to be a reliable partner on patrol.
